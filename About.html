About Me

Hello there! I am Sandy, a transitional UX/UI Designer, with a background in specialty coffee operations. Currently based in Berlin, Germany. I always have a passion for good coffee and have been working in specialty cafes/ roasteries since 2013 in different countries (from Hong Kong, to Melbourne, Vancouver and, finally, here in Berlin). Working frontend in the hospitality industry, it is a vision to create a better customer experience and, also, to build a greater coffee community for the baristas, cafe owners and coffee enthusiasts.

With the experience in hospitality, I have developed a customer-centric and teamworking mindset. In 2020, due to the global pandemic situation, an opportunity came up - the UX / UI Design course from CareerFoundry; that transferred the mindset to the digital and design aspect. I have acquired and practiced the following skills and knowledge for certain tools:
Skills:
  • User Research and developing user personas
  • Prototyping
  • Creating user flow and journey mapping
  • Usability test and data analysis
  • Establishing style guide and design system
Tools:
  • Adobe illustrator, Photoshop & XD
  • Figma (prototyping)
  • Principle (animation)
  • Frontend descriptive coding (HTML, CSS & Javascript)
